Our Story

It all started back in 2012,when booking needs for an event was a Big Unsolved Problem. That is when the idea of starting a one-stop portal, which will cater all event-related services struck our Einsteins. Thereafter, there was no looking back. Formerly known as Urbanrestro, Evenko Agency has today paved its way to become India's leading portal for booking Venues and other Vendors such as Decorators, Photographers and more. Our company's prime motto is to offer Easier, Smarter and Quicker experience of booking events that too at Unbelievable Rates! So be it a social or corporate event - Share your enquiry, shortlist your favourite venues and vendors and get the best deal for your event.

Our Mission

Evenko Agency is a global platform for live experiences that allows anyone to create, share, find and attend events that fuel their passions and enrich their lives. From music festivals, marathons, conferences, community rallies, and fundraisers, to gaming competitions and air guitar contests. Our mission is to bring the world together through live experiences.
